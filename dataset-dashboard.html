7 — Recommendations
  • TEI 1.8 (FlashBert) for MiniLM and Nomic models (apply cuda compat fix for container startup)
  • vLLM for jina-v5 -- only viable backend for this model
  • M2 MPS for budget-constrained runs -- free compute, good for smaller datasets (see throughput table)
  • TEI 1.8 L40S best throughput for MiniLM (6,252 chunks/s at $1.95/hr, 48GB VRAM) and strong for Nomic (513 chunks/s)
  • TEI 1.8 A10G best cost/performance ratio for Nomic embeddings ($1.00/hr, 260 chunks/s)
  • vLLM L40S best throughput for jina-nano (1,674 chunks/s at $1.95/hr)
  • vLLM A100 best throughput for jina-v5 (296.8 chunks/s at $2.00/hr)
  • pylate L40S for ColBERT models: mxbai-colbert (2,160 c/s) and answerai-colbert (2,092 c/s)
